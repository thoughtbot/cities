# Washington

## Coffee shops

These places have good coffee, good WiFi, and are easily accessible from public
transportation.

Ordered by their Metro stop:

* Adams Morgan/Woodley Park: Tryst (awesome but a little walk from Woodley)
* Chinatown: Dolcezza, La Colombe (nice space, no WiFi)
* Dupont Circle: Filter
* Eastern Market (Capitol Hill): Peregrine Espresso
* McPherson Square: Swigs (just like on _The West Wing_!)
* Union Station: Ebenezer's
* U Street: Slipstream

## User groups

We've been to and can recommend:

* [DCRUG](http://www.meetup.com/dcruby/), Second Thursday of the month are two
  talks, last Tuesday of the month is Hack Night
* [Arlington Ruby](http://www.meetup.com/Arlington-Ruby/)
* [United Silver Spring Ruby (USSR)](http://www.meetup.com/United-Silver-Spring-Ruby/)
* DC's JavaScript meetup is moribund. There are lots of more specific meetups in
  town for subsections of JS, particularly [React DC](http://www.meetup.com/React-DC/), which is now hosted by SocialTables
